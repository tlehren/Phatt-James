About

Phatt James is a melodic grunge rock band based out of Bridgeport, Connecticut. Their sound is influenced by a number of artists across the board however, often found comparable to the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Incubus, and Highly Suspect. The band includes Andrew DelGrosso on keys, Matt Christoforo on bass, Chris Wilson on drums, and Matthew James on vocals and guitar. They’ve recently released there debut EP, Circle Bent, and look forward to sharing more music.
